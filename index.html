Meet Florence

Who I Am

A passionate, Certified Sisterlocks Consultant dedicated to the art of natural hair and empowering my clients through meticulous care.

My love for Sisterlocks began with my own transformation. This personal experience inspired me to pursue official training in 2018 to share that same confidence with you.

I promise a personalized, supportive experience. We'll work together to achieve your hair goals in a comfortable and professional space.
